Car wrap refers to a large vinyl graphic that is applied to the surface of a vehicle. It's a popular way to change the appearance of a car without resorting to a permanent paint job. Car wraps can be used for aesthetic purposes, like changing the color or adding custom designs, or for advertising, where businesses use wraps to display logos and promotional messages.
